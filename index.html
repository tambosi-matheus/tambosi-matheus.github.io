Who am I?

My name is Matheus Henrique Tambosi, I am a 20 years old brazilian Game Developer living in Curitiba, PR. Currently enrolled in PUCPR Curitiba as a Game Development major with focus in programming skills.

I have several experiences ranging from programming to media management. I love working with teams and have a strong leadership and public speaking background.

As an avid gamer and follower of the competitive scene, I dream about one day being part for the amazing esports scene.

Here's a bit more
about me

I had my first contact with games at a very young age as my older sister had a Playstation 1, and when I got my first notebook at the age of 11, I played a competitive online game for the fist time and was completely hooked.

League of Legends was the first game that introduced me to esports and the pro scene. While I loved playing the game itself, I couldn't - and still can't nowadays - resist watching and following teams. Even seven years after, I can see clear as day Impact's Jax top walking through every oponent in the 2013 Worlds Finals

Late in my 9th grade (2015), I was invited by one of my friends to MundoCMC, my school's Model United Nations

Moving foward, when I was in my first year of high school, I saw the opportunity to join an esports organization as a player , but it wasn't my fit, and some monthes later as my classes came back I left the org. Later that same year, I got in contact with a teacher that let me had free programming classes in the local IFPR.

In 2017, I was one of the few selected by my school to have classes in UTFPR about various subjects, such as Gliders, Robotics I & II and Game Development. The last one was when my mind was blown out ... you can actually work making games?!

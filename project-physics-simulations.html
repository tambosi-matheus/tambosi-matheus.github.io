Physics Simulations

A compilation of small prototypes developed for my 'Movements, Explosions & Particles for games' class

Repository link with all projects

Follow

A demonstration of the Uniform Motion pattern. By clicking in the canvas, the spaceship moves to the target selected by the mouse, rotating in 0.5 secs and moving to the position in 2 seconds.

Landing

Implementation of acceleration and gravity with collision check in the boundaries, use your mouse click to acceleracte the spaceship to the mouse's position.

Collisions

Example of two basic collision checks, with square collision box and with circle collision. Use Q and E to add squares and Circles respectively, and use mouse click to add points to the canvas. Red points means a collision occurred.

Collisions Box2D

A side project that I made because I found Box2D while studying for the class. It's a basic implementation of the framework, with platforms changing colors when colliding with the player. Use WASD to control the ball.

Collision Grid

Implementation of the Collision Grid pattern with movement prediction for handling multiple objects and collisions. When objects collide they change colors. They also slow down a little bit as they have a coefficient of restitution. Use the mouse click to add more objects, and R to reset the simulation.

Forces

A more robust implementation of a forces system, with the usage of mass. External forces include gravity and wind. Use WASD to move the spaceship.

Birds

A simulation of the Angry Birds slingshot physics. Use mouse and mouseclick to control the spaceship while grounded, and R to reset the simulation.

Water

Usage of a friction system with different materials that helps to visualize force loss in a system and its impact.

Universal Gravitation

Usage of Newton's law of Universal Gravitation in a system with two planets and a Spaceship. The lower planet has the biggest mass (aka attracts more) and the force is a hundred times stronger in the ship for demonstration purposes.

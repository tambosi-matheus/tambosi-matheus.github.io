AAEE RedRex

Some years after learning about university pro scene I got into PUCPR and discovered their esports org, AAEE RedRex. As I was just entering the university, I preferred to first get used to my new routine, and soon after try to join the org.

July 2019, after finishing my first semester at the university, I sent a message to the org was soon integrated to the board of directors. Getting used to the org's routine was quite easy, as I already knew a pro team training routine and had some leadership experience with my volunteer jobs

By the end of the year, I was already a established and active member, which led me being elected Vice-President of the organization for the 2020 academic year, and apointed as League Of Legends Team Manager.

Within the time I'm in org, we have already won several championships stateswide and even nationwide.

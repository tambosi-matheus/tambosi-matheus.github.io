Poke the Ogre

A prototype being developed at LAJE, it's based on the Dota 2's mod "Poke the Angry Ogre". You are one of the town's champions that are fighting against the giant ogre. Be the one to kill him and conquer all the glory!

With a team of two - me as programmer, and Pedro as game designer, the project is in slow pace, but never out!

The first big challange of this one was the multiplayer, where I spent some weeks studying Photon Bolt framework and implementing it correctly in Unity<./p>

Now we're heading to the development of the main gameloop to present a basic prototype by October 30th

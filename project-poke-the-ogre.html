Poke the Ogre

A prototype being developed at LAJE, it's based on the Dota 2's mod "Poke the Angry Ogre". You are one of the town's champions that are fighting against the giant ogre. Be the one to kill him and conquer all the glory!

With a team of three - me as programmer, Pedro as game designer, and Lion as 3D artist, the main focus of the game was studying and understanding the multiplayer technology (in this case, Photon Bolt was chosen), and helping the feshman Pedro to adapt to the studio.

With 3~4 months developing the game, we think we got what we needed from the project and will probably close it by the end of October. For such a small team, we found out that making an online game was quite out of reach, and we and our professors thought it was a better idea to move on to another project.

Discovering University Pro League

As I was doing some extracurricular activities at the local university, one of my teachers invited me to the majors fair that was happening in the other side of the university campus. I knew that I love programming and I'd probably try to land on a STEM related course, such as computer engineering or computer science. So I went to the fair just to take a quick peek at those courses standings.

As I browsed through, one thing caught my attention, some guys dressed in black & blue uniforms in the near-end of the exposition. When I came a little closer, I saw a small TV on the standing table showing some League of Legends clip.

That's when I discovered Azure Jay Esports (later renamed Avalanche Esports) and the whole university pro scene. Before that, my only personal contact with Brazil's esport scene was when I played for a Tier 3 team and in soloq. I was suprised, but more than that, happy that esports as a whole has grown this much.

Later, as I went home, I started stalking the local leagues and teams, and this occasion that led me joining RedRex later when I joined PUCPR.
